Acknowledgements

This online manufacturing simulation was originally developed by Daniel Kovalevich, Zachary Gabel, Saaman Khalilollahi, and overseen by Dr. Faisal Aqlan and Dr. Hui Yang, from Penn State University. The project has since been modified by Cameron Butts, Cameron coursey, and Nicholas Dipasquale. The objective of the program is to use car toys for teaching the basics of Lean manufacturing. Simulations can be practical teaching aids that help students understand real industrial practices and provide a space for application of theories and ideas learned in the classroom.
